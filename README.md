# 🚀 FinSight – Customer Intelligence & Decision Engine

## 🧠 Overview
FinSight is a decision-driven analytics system that transforms raw customer data into actionable business insights.

Built for the Blostem AI Builder Hackathon (Data Analytics & Insights track), FinSight goes beyond visualization by combining segmentation, risk analysis, and decision logic into a unified system.

---

## 🎯 Problem
Businesses today collect large amounts of customer data but struggle to convert it into meaningful decisions. This leads to:
- Missed revenue opportunities  
- High customer churn  
- Generic, ineffective engagement strategies  

---

## 💡 Solution
FinSight bridges this gap by turning customer data into a **decision engine**.

It enables businesses to:
- Understand customer segments  
- Identify churn risk  
- Detect revenue opportunities  
- Take targeted, data-driven actions  

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 📊 Customer Segmentation
Clusters customers based on income and behavioral patterns to identify distinct user groups.

### ⚠️ Churn Risk Analysis
Classifies customers into:
- High Risk  
- Medium Risk  
- Low Risk  

### 💰 Revenue Opportunity Scoring
Identifies high-value customers who are currently under-engaged.

### 🧩 Decision Engine
Transforms insights into clear actions:

- High Opportunity + High Risk → Immediate Action  
- High Opportunity + Low Risk → Growth Focus  
- Low Opportunity + High Risk → Low Priority
